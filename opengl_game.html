OpenGL Project

Game Overview

  • Explore the map and fight enemies
  • Move and rotate camera with joysticks
  • While in combat press A to attack, B to defend and X to heal (Xbox360 controller)

Technical Overview

  • Languages: C++ and GLSL
  • Libraries: SFML, Assimp, Bullet, GLM
  • Notable Features: GPU Skinning, Shadow Mapping, Toon Shading
  • Tools: Blender (modelling, rigging, texturing and animating)
  • Platforms: Linux

Notes

Requires a gamepad to play
